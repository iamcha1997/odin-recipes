Chicken Arroz Caldo

(Filipino Chicken and Rice Soup)


Description

This Filipino Arroz Caldo recipe or Filipino lugaw is comfort in a bowl. Similar to Chinese congee, this classic comfort food is a Filipino rice porridge made with chicken and flavored with ginger, garlic, onion, and a touch of fish sauce for umami.


Ingredients

  • 700 grams of chicken
  • 1 cup of uncooked white or glutinous rice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil or coconut oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely diced
  • 3 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 2-3 tablespoons of finely chopped ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 7 cups of water
  • 2 organic chicken bouillon cubes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to your liking
  • For Serving, consider:
    • 4 hard-boiled chicken eggs or quail eggs (optional)
    • 4 teaspoons of crispy fried garlic
    • 2-3 scallions, finely sliced
    • Lemon or Calamansi wedges
    • Extra fish sauce

Instruction

  • Hard Boiled Eggs:
    1. Boil Eggs for 8-10 mins., let it cool in an ice water bath.
    2. Peel off eggshells then slice in half & set aside.
    3. Heat 4 tbsp. of oil in a pan on a low heat.
  • Fried Garlic:
    1. Mince 1 bulb of garlic & fry until golden brown.
    2. Strain fried garlic from oil & set aside separately.
  • Arroz Caldo:
    1. Heat 3 tbsp. of garlic oil in a large pot.
    2. Sauté sliced ginger & onions until softened.
    3. Sear chicken drumsticks for 3 mins.
    4. Season with Black Pepper & Chicken Bouillon Cube.
    5. Simmer chicken for 8mins. until renders chicken stock.
    6. Add washed & soaked Glutinous & Jasmine Rice, then sauté for 3 mins.
    7. Add 5 cups of water, cover & bring to a boil.
    8. Stir occasionally and simmer for 20 mins. on med. low heat.
    9. Season with 1tsp. of Turmeric powder & 2tbsp. of fish sauce, to taste.
    10. Add 1cup water & simmer for another 12-15mins.
    11. Taste Test, season with Fish Sauce & Black Pepper, adjust according to your preference.
    12. Serve in a bowl and top with Hard Boiled Egg, Fried Garlic, Spring Onions.
