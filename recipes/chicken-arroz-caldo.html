Chicken Arroz Caldo

(Filipino Chicken and Rice Soup)


Description

This Filipino Arroz Caldo recipe or Filipino lugaw is comfort in a bowl. Similar to Chinese congee, this classic comfort food is a Filipino rice porridge made with chicken and flavored with ginger, garlic, onion, and a touch of fish sauce for umami.


Ingredients

  • 700 grams of chicken
  • 1 cup of uncooked white or glutinous rice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil or coconut oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely diced
  • 3 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 2-3 tablespoons of finely chopped ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 7 cups of water
  • 2 organic chicken bouillon cubes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to your liking
  • For Serving, consider:
    • 4 hard-boiled chicken eggs or quail eggs (optional)
    • 4 teaspoons of crispy fried garlic
    • 2-3 scallions, finely sliced
    • Lemon or Calamansi wedges
    • Extra fish sauce
